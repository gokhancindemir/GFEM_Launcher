User Guide & FAQ

📌 Shortcut Operations

  • Adding:
    You can easily add and categorize your content using the 'Add XXX' buttons in the top menu.(Shortcut names are limited to a maximum of 15 characters).
  • Editing:
    You can right-click any card to modify.
  • Visualization:
    You can assign images in .png formats as icons using the 'Change Icon' option.
  • Right-Click Menu:
    Right-click on any shortcut card to access full control. Through this context menu, you can:
  • Open the file location,
  • Rename the shortcut,
  • Move it to a different tab or group,
  • Assign a custom icon to the card,
  • Delete the shortcut.

💻 System & Hardware

  • Drive Tracking:
    You can monitor the occupancy rates of your drives live in the 'My PC' tab.Data is updated on every entry or by clicking the 'Refresh' button.
  • Auto-Start:
    Enable 'Run on Startup' from Settings to have the program ready when your computer turns on.

🎨 Appearance & Personalization

  • Font Family:
    You can choose from professional fonts like Montserrat or Roboto to improve readability.
  • Theme Mode:
    You can use 'Dark Mode' to reduce eye strain or 'Light Mode' for a classic look.
  • Accent Color:
    Personalize the active menus, buttons, and lines of the application with your favorite color.
  • Window Behavior & Quick Minimize:
    To ensure a seamless workflow and allow consecutive clicks, the Launcher window is designed to always stay on top of other applications. Once you are done, simply right-click on any empty space within the interface to instantly minimize the window to the taskbar.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will my shortcuts be deleted if I uninstall?
A: No, your data is stored safely. We recommend backing up before formatting.

Q: Why can't I see the icons?
A: The source file or icon path might have changed. Re-select the icon to fix it.

Q: How do I reset the program completely?
A: You can clear all data and preferences using the 'Reset to Defaults' button in the Settings menu.

Q: Will my appearance settings be reset when I close the program?
A: No, your selected font, theme, and color settings are saved to local storage and are loaded according to your preferences every time the application starts.

Q: How can I reorganize shortcuts and change their groups?
A: You can rearrange shortcuts by dragging them left or right within a group, or move them to a different group by dragging them onto the desired group header.

Q: Can I create shortcuts by dragging and dropping files from outside the app?
A: No. For security and system stability, dragging and dropping external files is disabled. Please use the 'Add' buttons within the menu to create new shortcuts.
